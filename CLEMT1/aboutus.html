ABOUT US

In a Nutshell: We fight extreme poverty.

MISSION

The Stop Poverty statement is Give to care and Care to give. Our mission is to provide the basic needs of those, who can not provide for themselfs (or are not provided for by other means) and help them to become self-reliant and lead a decent life in dignity. The Stop Poverty projects are focused on the basic needs of people, like Education and Health. At the same time our projects always aim at sustainability by realising local money generating initiatives like agriculture projects, micro finances and vocational training programs. The goal is, that these communities can continue to provide for their own population and children in health and education.

VISION

The vision of Stop Poverty PH is to contribute to the eraddication of poverty in the Phlippines. This is a realistic goal. We have good news.The decline of poverty in the Philippines is amazing.By our integral way of working in Work and Income, Education and Health, Stop Poverty PH accomplishes sustainable community projects.With a clear beginning (sponsorship) and a clear ending (financially self-sustainable).
