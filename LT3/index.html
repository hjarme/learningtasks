My Top 10 Favorite Marvel Movies

Top 1

Thor Ragnarok

Imprisoned on the planet Sakaar, Thor must race against time to return to Asgard and stop Ragnarök, the destruction of his world, at the hands of the powerful and ruthless villain Hela

Top 2

Shang-Chi

Shang-Chi, who must confront the past he thought he left behind when he is drawn into the web of the mysterious Ten Rings organization

Top 3

Iron Man 3

Iron Man against an enemy whose reach knows no bounds. When Stark finds his personal world destroyed at his enemy's hands, he embarks on a harrowing quest to find those responsible.

Top 4

Black Panther

Black Panther (T’Challa) the King of Wakanda after the death of his father returns home to the isolated, technologically advanced African nation to succeed to the throne and take his place as king.

Top 5

Avengers Infinity War

As the Avengers and their allies have continued to protect the world from threats too large for any one hero to handle, a new danger has emerged from the cosmic shadows: Thanos.

Top 6

Wonder Woman

When a pilot crashes and tells of conflict in the outside world, Diana, an Amazonian warrior in training, leaves home to fight a war, discovering her full powers and true destiny.

Top 7

Spider-Man 2

Spider-Man must fight Doctor Octopus but he has also decided to follow his love of Mary Jane Watson . It is the sequel to 2002's Spider-Man and followed by Spider-Man 3.

Top 8

Hulk

Bruce Banner, a scientist on the run from the U.S. Government, must find a cure for the monster he turns into whenever he loses his temper.

Top 9

Eternals

The saga of the Eternals, a race of immortal beings who lived on Earth and shaped its history and civilizations.
